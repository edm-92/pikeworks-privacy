Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 April 2026

Deep Focus ("the App") is developed and published by Pikeworks ("we", "us", "our"). This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All app data is stored locally on your device.

However, the App uses third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob — serves advertisements and may collect your device's advertising ID, approximate location (via IP address), app usage data, and diagnostic information.
  • Google Play Billing — processes in-app purchases. Purchase data is handled entirely by Google.

2. How Data Is Used

  • Advertising ID: Used by AdMob to serve relevant advertisements
  • App usage data: Used by AdMob for analytics and ad targeting
  • Diagnostic data: Used to improve ad delivery performance

All data collected by third-party services is encrypted in transit via TLS.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — advertising
  • Google Play Billing — in-app purchases

4. GDPR Compliance

For users in the EEA and United Kingdom, we obtain consent before serving personalised advertisements via the Google UMP SDK. You can change your consent preferences at any time in the App's Settings screen.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

6. Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our servers. Data collected by AdMob is subject to Google's privacy policy.

7. Your Rights

  • Opt out of personalised ads via device settings (Settings > Google > Ads)
  • Reset your advertising ID at any time
  • Change GDPR consent in the App's Settings screen
  • Uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
