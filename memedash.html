Meme Dash — Privacy Policy

Last updated 1 May 2026

What this app is

Meme Dash is an arcade tap-to-flap game published by Pikeworks. This policy explains exactly what data the app collects, why, and how you control it.

Effective date

Last updated 1 May 2026. We will update this page whenever the policy changes — there is no separate version inside the app you need to check.

Data collected directly by us

None. Meme Dash stores your high score, coins, character collection, login streak, season pass progress and settings on your device only. We do not have a server and we do not have your personal data.

Data collected via Google AdMob

Meme Dash shows banner and rewarded video adverts via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Approximate location (derived from your IP address)
  • Advertising ID (a resettable identifier you control in Android Settings)
  • App interactions (advert impressions, clicks, view duration)
  • Device information (model, OS version, network type)
  • Crash and diagnostic data

This data is used only to serve adverts and to keep the AdMob service running. AdMob's full policy is at policies.google.com/privacy.

GDPR and your consent

If you live in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, AdMob will ask for your consent before showing personalised adverts. You can change your decision at any time from Settings → Privacy Settings inside Meme Dash.

In-app purchases

Meme Dash offers optional one-time purchases (Remove Ads, coin packs, the Coin Doubler, the Mystery Skin, the Starter Pack, the Brainrot Pass and the Premium Bundle). Payments are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We never see your card details — only an anonymous purchase token confirming the transaction.

Children

Meme Dash is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has used the app, contact us and we will help where we can — but as we have no servers, no account data exists to delete.

Your rights

  • Reset your advertising ID at any time in Android Settings → Privacy → Ads
  • Withdraw advert consent in Meme Dash Settings → Privacy Settings
  • Buy the Remove Ads or Premium Bundle IAP to stop banner adverts entirely
  • Uninstall the app, which deletes every byte of your local data
  • Email us with any data question and expect a reply within 30 days

Third parties

Only three third-party SDKs ship with Meme Dash:

  • Google AdMob — adverts (advert IDs, impressions, IP)
  • Google Play Billing — IAP transactions (purchase tokens only)
  • Google Play Games Services — optional online leaderboard (only if you sign in to Play Games on your device; only your high score is submitted, never personal data)

We do not use Firebase Analytics, Facebook SDK, or any other tracking library.

Contact

Questions, complaints, or requests? Email pikeworksapps@gmail.com — we read every message and reply within 30 days. There is no postal address required for any GDPR right; email is sufficient.
