Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 April 2026

Grove: Garden & Orchard Tracker ("the App") is developed and published by Pikeworks ("we", "us", "our"). This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All plant, garden, harvest, and task data is stored locally on your device only.

The App uses the following device capabilities:

  • Location (approximate) — Used solely to fetch weather data from the Open-Meteo API for your local area. Your location is sent directly to Open-Meteo and is never stored by Pikeworks.
  • Internet — Used only to fetch weather forecast data from the Open-Meteo API. No personal data is transmitted.
  • Camera/Photos — Used to attach photos to your plant records. Photos are stored locally on your device and never uploaded to any server.
  • Notifications — Used for task reminders (e.g. pruning, watering). All notification scheduling is local.

2. No Advertising, No Tracking

Grove is a paid app. It contains no advertisements, no analytics SDKs, no tracking pixels, and no in-app purchases. We do not use Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics, or any similar service.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Open-Meteo — free weather API. Your approximate location (latitude/longitude) is sent to retrieve weather data. Open-Meteo does not require authentication and does not track users. See their terms.
  • Google Play Review API — used to prompt for app reviews. No personal data is collected by our app through this API.

4. Data Storage

All your data — plants, gardens, harvests, photos, tasks, and settings — is stored in a local database on your device. This data is never uploaded to any server. If you uninstall the App, all data is permanently deleted.

5. GDPR Compliance

Since we do not collect or process personal data, GDPR data subject requests are not applicable. However, if you have any concerns, please contact us.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

  • Deny location permission to prevent weather data fetching
  • Deny notification permission to prevent task reminders
  • Export your data as CSV from the App's Settings screen
  • Uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
